Description

Pizza! You know what it is. Everyone loves pizza. Neopolitan style, Chicago style, New York style, Detroit style. Even St. Louis style, with it's cracker thin crust. Hot pizza for dinner, cold pizza for breakfast, a quick slice for lunch. Who doesn't love pizza? Perfect for a meal on the go or a fancy sit-down dinner. Pizza, it's whats for dinner, lunch, breakfast, second breakfast ...

Ingredients

  • dough
  • cheese
  • sauce
  • pepperoni (optional)

Instructions

  1. roll out dough
  2. spread sauce over dough
  3. sprinkle cheese over sauce
  4. place pepperoni over cheese if desired
  5. cook in oven
